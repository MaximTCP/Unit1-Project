Project Management in Social Media

Presented by Maxim Sevcenco

Unit 1 Project | Ms. Triantis | Class Period: 2nd

The Project Manager Role

  • Orchestrates social media campaigns from concept to completion
  • Aligns creative vision with business objectives
  • Manages timelines, budgets, and team coordination
  • Acts as bridge between clients, creatives, and executives

Becoming a PM

Education Path

  • Marketing or Business Degrees
  • PMP or CAPM Certifications
  • Social Media Marketing Courses

Experience Needed

  • 3-5 years in digital marketing
  • Proven campaign leadership
  • Platform expertise (Meta, TikTok, etc.)

"At NextEast Media, we value hands-on experience with influencer campaigns and viral content strategies as much as formal education."

The PM Experience

Challenges

  • Managing creative egos and client expectations
  • Platform algorithm changes
  • 24/7 nature of social media
  • Tight deadlines with high stakes

Satisfaction

  • Seeing campaigns go viral
  • Building brand communities
  • Creative problem solving
  • Tangible ROI results

"The thrill of launching a successful campaign outweighs all challenges."
